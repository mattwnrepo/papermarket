How PaperMarket Works
📈 Prediction Markets

A prediction market lets you bet on whether a real-world event will happen. Each question has two outcomes — YES and NO — and the price of each reflects the crowd's estimated probability.

Prices are expressed in cents and always sum to roughly 100¢. If YES trades at 70¢, the market thinks there's a ~70% chance the event happens.

YES 70¢ → 70% likely NO 30¢ → 30% likely
🛒 Shares, Stake & P&L
Stake
The amount of USDC you spend on a trade. Type it into the stake field on each market card.
Shares
What you receive in return. Each share pays out $1.00 if your chosen outcome wins, $0 if it loses. Shares = Stake ÷ Price.
Price per share
The current market price in cents (e.g. 70¢). Cheaper shares mean the market considers that outcome less likely — but the payout is the same $1.00.
Limit Order
A buy order that only fills when the price drops to or below your target. Cash is only deducted when it triggers on the next data refresh.
P&L
Profit & Loss. Current value of your position minus what you paid for it. Goes up if the market moves in your favour.
